Chris Whittaker

I am an historic environmental specialist with technical expertise in geospatial analysis, data management and remote sensing.

I research historic land management practices to help inform modern day environmental decision-making.

Such practices include:

  • Common rights
  • Sustainable woodland and tree management (e.g. wood pasture systems, coppicing, bollarding)
  • Sustainable building and materials
  • Intelligent flood mitigation
  • Green boundaries: hedgerows and their many values
